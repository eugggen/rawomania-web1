Welcome to RAWomania

A bold, joyful, and soulful gathering.

A one-day event in the heart of Bucharest where the raw, the real, and the beautifully imperfect come together in a celebration of living wines.

Think of it as a collective inhale.

Curious drinkers meet daring winemakers, stories are told across tables, and wines express origin, care, chaos in just the right balance.

Whether you've been part of this movement for decades or you're showing your first bottled daydreams, RAWomania is a space made for encounters.
